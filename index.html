Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the admission process?
Registration forms are available online and at the school office. After submission, an interaction/assessment is scheduled.
What board follows the school's curriculum?
We follow the CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education) curriculum for all grades from Foundation to Higher Secondary.
What are the school timings?
Primary Section: 8:30 AM - 1:30 PM. Secondary Section: 8:00 AM - 2:30 PM.
Does the school provide transport?
Yes, we have a fleet of safe, GPS-enabled buses covering major routes in the city.
